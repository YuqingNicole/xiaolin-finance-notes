01 / 10

风险的本质：风险 ≠ 波动率

风险是永久性资本损失的概率，不是价格波动。风险最高的时候，恰恰是所有人觉得最安全的时候——安全感催生不审慎的行为。

Riskier investments absolutely cannot be counted on to deliver higher returns. Why not? It's simple: if riskier investments reliably produced higher returns, they wouldn't be riskier!

— Risk (2006)

The riskiest thing in the world is the widespread belief that there's no risk.

— It's Not Easy (2015)

One of my favorite adages concerns the six-foot-tall man who drowned crossing the stream that was five feet deep on average.

— Volatility + Leverage = Dynamite (2008)
⚠️
风险的悖论性
所有人觉得安全 → 行为不审慎 → 真实风险最高。恐慌蔓延 → 价格暴跌 → 风险已被释放。
⚡
杠杆是炸药
杠杆不改变对错概率，只放大后果。LTCM 自称"在全世界捡硬币"——省略了"在压路机前面"。
⚖️
风险不可回避
不愿承担风险本身就是极其冒险的策略。投资者的工作是聪明地承担风险以获取回报。
02 / 10

周期与钟摆：万物皆有周期

市场情绪像钟摆，在极端之间摆动，几乎从不停留在中间。你不能预测周期的时点，但必须知道身处周期的什么位置。

The mood swings of the securities markets resemble the movement of a pendulum. Although the midpoint of its arc best describes the location of the pendulum "on average," it actually spends very little of its time there.

— First Quarter Performance (1991)

The worst loans are made at the best of times.

— You Can't Predict. You Can Prepare. (2001)
🔄
牛市三阶段
① 少数前瞻者觉得会好转
② 多数人确认改善正在发生
③ 所有人认为会永远好下去
🌊
Sea Change (2022)
40年利率下降趋势结束。这不是周期波动，是根本性转变。投资者过去走在"机场传送带"上，现在传送带停了。
🌡️
测温度，不预报天气
50年只做了5次市场判断，每次都在极端时刻。"Yeah, Dad, that's because you did it five times in 50 years." — Andrew Marks
03 / 10

预测的无效与知识的谦逊

"I know" 学派与 "I don't know" 学派。Marks 坚定地站在后者——承认你不知道的，比假装你知道的更有投资价值。

There are two kinds of forecasters: those who don't know, and those who don't know they don't know.

— Galbraith, 引于 The Illusion of Knowledge (2022)

Human beings want to feel that they are on a power walk into the future, when in fact we are always just tapping our canes on the pavement in the fog.

— Uncertainty II (2020)

95% of all financial history happens within two standard deviations of normal, and everything interesting happens outside of two standard deviations.

— Uncertainty II (2020)
04 / 10

逆向投资：敢于不同

杰出投资需要非共识且正确的判断。二层思维是核心能力：不仅要有判断，还要判断共识的判断。

This just in: you can't take the same actions as everyone else and expect to outperform.

— Dare to Be Great (2006)

I'm convinced that everything that's important in investing is counterintuitive, and everything that's obvious is wrong.

— Dare to Be Great II (2014)

Dare to Be Great 矩阵

平均好成绩常规行为 × 有利结果
⭐ 超额回报非常规行为 × 有利结果
平均差成绩常规行为 × 不利结果
超额亏损非常规行为 × 不利结果
← 常规行为非常规行为 →
05 / 10

运气、技能与决策质量

决策质量不能由结果判断。好决策可能因坏运气失败，坏决策可能因好运气成功。

Success in gambling doesn't go to those who pick winners, but to those with the ability to identify superior propositions — mispricing.

— You Bet! (2020)

$10 million earned through Russian roulette does not have the same value as $10 million earned through the diligent and artful practice of dentistry.

— Nassim Taleb, 引于 Returns and How They Get That Way (2002)

Risk means more things can happen than will happen.

— Elroy Dimson, 引于 Getting Lucky (2014)
06 / 10

价格与价值：买得好 > 买好的

没有任何资产好到不能被高价毁掉，也没有任何资产差到不能被低价拯救。

In the end, superior investing is all about mistakes... and about being the person who profits from them, not the one who commits them.

— It's All a Big Mistake (2012)

The first rule of compounding is to never interrupt it unnecessarily.

— Charlie Munger, 引于 Something of Value (2021)
💰
RCA 教训
1927-1929 从 $8 涨到 $114，之后跌至 $2.5。收音机确实改变了世界，但投资者仍巨亏。
📈
价值/成长融合
2021年 Something of Value 标志着 Marks 的思想演化：对于真正的复利机器，应该"说服自己不要卖"。
🎯
利用错误
Bruce Karsh 困境债团队 23 年平均 23% 年化回报。对手犯错是其商业模式根基。
07 / 10

投资者心理：从"完美"到"绝望"一步之遥

投资的基础是心理学，不是金融学。市场没有特殊智慧，价格下跌是反映性的，不是预测性的。

In the real world, things generally fluctuate between "pretty good" and "not so hot." But in the world of investing, perception often swings from "flawless" to "hopeless."

— On the Couch (2016)

If I could know only one thing about an investment I'm contemplating, it might be how much optimism is embodied in the price.

— On the Couch (2016)
08 / 10

防守型投资：避免失败优先

"If we avoid the losers, the winners will take care of themselves." 平庸中的一致性创造卓越。

The key to investment success isn't hitting home runs; it's avoiding strikeouts and inning-ending double plays.

— What's Your Game Plan? (2003)

There's such a thing as the risk of taking too little risk.

— Fewer Losers, or More Winners? (2023)

Tom Kite
20年累计奖金最多却从未赢过大满贯。持续稳定"进钱圈"胜过偶尔夺冠。
⚾
Henry Aaron
最佳赛季不如 Bonds/Mays，但"他在最高水平上打得比任何人都久"。

养老金奇迹

09 / 10

不对称性：卓越投资的终极标准

上涨时跟上市场，下跌时少亏。短期业绩不重要，5-10 年的不对称性才是真正的 alpha。

Don't just do something; sit there.

— I Beg to Differ (2022)

It's one or the other. You can't simultaneously emphasize both preservation of capital and maximization of growth. This is the fundamental, inescapable truth in investing.

— Ruminating on Asset Allocation (2024)
